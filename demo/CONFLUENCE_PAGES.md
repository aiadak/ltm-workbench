# Confluence pages to create for the show-and-tell

Create these 4 pages in your Confluence space **Workbench_SME**. Total time: ~10 minutes.

For each: click **+ Create** (top nav) → **Blank page** → paste the title and body → **Publish**.

Set the page **Labels** using the ⋯ menu at the top of the published page → **Add labels**.

---

## Page 1 — Panel-cause language tells

- **Title:** `Panel-cause fires — signature language tells (adjuster field notes)`
- **Labels:** `fire, panel-cause, adjuster-notes, training`
- **Body (paste as-is; Confluence will format headings):**

```
Signature tells

Three FNOL phrases reliably signal panel-cause across the last two years of files:

- "popping sound before the smoke"
- "lights flickered before we saw fire"
- fire location described as electrical service entrance or main breaker panel area

Confirmation path

1. Order origin report immediately
2. Cross-reference model against active recall list on receipt
3. Preserve panel physically — chain of custody required for subrogation

Do not skip preservation

Even on small files. Even without Endorsement 4A. The recall subrogation path is
available regardless of endorsement. Preserving the panel is cheap; burning subro
because you did not is expensive.
```

## Page 2 — ROR-before-letter phone call

- **Title:** `ROR-before-letter phone call — script and objections`
- **Labels:** `ror, customer-communication, correspondence, training`
- **Body:**

```
The rule

Never let a Reservation of Rights letter arrive cold. Call the insured first,
explain the ROR in plain language, then send the letter within 24 hours.

The script

"This is a big claim, our engineer has to look at the panel, and while he does
we are keeping our options open, but I have zero reason to think anything is
wrong. When the report comes back clean, I will call you the same day and we
will pay."

Common objections

Objection: "Are you saying I did this on purpose?"
Response: "Not at all. ROR is a coverage protection. It gives us time to look
at the panel without prejudicing your claim."

Objection: "Can you not just wait until you know?"
Response: "The letter has to be issued within a specific window. But nothing
about it changes your payment timing if origin comes back clean, which I fully
expect."

Objection: "I need contents ALE now."
Response: "We are extending contents ALE on the standard rolling basis. What
we are being careful about is the dwelling extension, because 4A opens that
exposure wide and I need to see origin before I can commit."
```

## Page 3 — Median-outcome reserving

- **Title:** `Reserve discipline — median-outcome reserving (Karolczak method)`
- **Labels:** `reserves, discipline, training, panel-cause`
- **Body:**

```
Set to median, not fear

Set reserve to the median outcome for the pattern you are seeing. Do not over-reserve
to feel safe. ALE decisions get slow when the reserve is inflated.

For the 4A + prior + panel triple

First-cut range: $60,000 to $75,000. Median: $68,000.

Review points

- Day 10 review is MANDATORY. Origin reports historically land between Day 7 and
  Day 12. Reserve drift after that point is expensive.
- Day 30 review is soft, absorbs subrogation-adjacent revisions.

Track record

Across 30 files handled in the last 24 months, reserve accuracy has landed within
8 percent of final on 27 of 30. The 3 misses were all wiring reclassifications —
which is why the wiring exception is called out separately in the playbook.
```

## Page 4 — The no-4A recall path (the junior mistake)

- **Title:** `The no-4A recall path — a common junior mistake`
- **Labels:** `fire, panel-cause, training, exception, subrogation`
- **Body:**

```
The mistake

Junior adjusters see a small fire with no Endorsement 4A on the policy and assume
there is no subrogation path. This is wrong.

The rule

Manufacturer recall subrogation is available on ANY panel-cause fire regardless of
endorsement. Endorsement 4A only affects dwelling extension exposure — not the
mechanical subro path.

The action

Preserve the panel on every panel-cause fire. Every one. Even the small ones. The
chain of custody is cheap; recovery is meaningful to the book.

Confirmation

Cross-reference the panel model against the active recall database (see the
Manufacturer Recall Database page in this space). If the model is in the recall
window, open a subrogation file the same day and notify the manufacturer via
certified mail.
```
